Ural Rex

The Ural Rex is a very rare breed. They were developed from a naturally occuring mutation. They have medium length coats and are curly-haired, like most of the Rex generation of cats. Their roots can be traced back to Russia. Unlike a lot of cats, the Ural Rex does not require much maintenance, like brushing, so if you are looking for a cat who does not demand to be brushed everyday, this is the perfect one.
